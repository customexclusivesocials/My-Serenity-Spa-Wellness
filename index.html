Our Story

Built from Jodi's own journey of transformation.

Jodi Speight opened her home-based business, My Serenity Spa, in 2004 after her own path of healing and growth. What began as one room has grown into a full practice — without ever losing that original, personal feel.

"Helping others feel good and guiding them to a happier lifestyle is my greatest reward. I love what I do, and I would love to share these experiences with you."

Today, Jodi personally performs every treatment — every appointment is time with the owner, not a rotating roster. As the practice grows, this is where the team will be introduced.
